🧠 Setup Guide & How It Works
Step 1 — Get Finnhub Key (Free)
Go to finnhub.io, sign up free, and copy your API key from the dashboard. Paste it in the "Finnhub API Key" box above. Free tier: 60 calls/min, real-time US stocks.
Step 2 — Real Data Flow
StockMind fetches live quotes + 12 months of daily candles from Finnhub. All ML indicators (RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, regression) are computed on real OHLCV data.
Step 3 — Claude AI (Optional)
Optionally add your Anthropic API key for Claude's qualitative market analysis. Real computed metrics are sent to Claude for nuanced BUY/HOLD/SELL reasoning.
Step 4 — 30-Min Auto-Refresh
Once analyzing, the app auto-refreshes real data every 30 minutes. A countdown timer shows time to next refresh. Hit "↻ Refresh Now" to force-update anytime.
